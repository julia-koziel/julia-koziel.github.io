About us

We believe that everyone is unique and One of a Kind! Our mission is to create an individual profile of (relative) strengths and difficulties for each child. We aim to accomplish that by developing touch-screen games that measure cognitive, emotional and social processes in children and young people of all ages and abilities. We are currently working on three applications, each intended for a different age group, spanning from as early as 3 years of age to early adulthood.

Pip's World

Pip’s World is an application for pre-schoolers who are between the ages of 3 and 6. It follows the life of Pip and his friends and includes 10 fun games, where children get to find magic coins or play with a puppy! We are looking for parents of children who would be interested to download the app and test the games with their child, all in the comfort of their home!

    • Time Trekkers

    Time Trekkers is an application for school-age children between the ages of 6 and 11. Theo, the main character in the game, has built a special time machine that can transport a child to play games in different eras in the past e.g. the Dinosaur or the Pirate era. We are currently in the intial stage of gathering feedback and we hope to find interested parents and children to take part in the Time Trekkers Study!

    • What can I do to help?

    Click on the tabs corresponding to different applications to find out more about studies that we are currently conducting. By taking part, you'll help us to develop these games further and ensure that they are reliable measures of children's cognitive, emotional and social functioning.
