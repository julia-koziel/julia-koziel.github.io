Time Trekkers Study

This is a website for our study run by researchers at King's College London and funded by the SFARI foundation. Explore this page to find our more about our research, find out how to participate and download the app.

    • How to participate?

    1. Send an email to timetrekkers-study@kcl.ac.uk to sign up.

    2. We will send you your child's participant ID to use in the application. We will also send you a link to fill in some questionnaires.

    3. Play the games with your child and complete the questionnaires. Send us an email when you're finished!

    4. We will send you an e-voucher as a form of reimbursement.
