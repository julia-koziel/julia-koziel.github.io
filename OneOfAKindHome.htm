About us

We believe that everyone is unique and One of a Kind! Our mission is to create an individual profile of (relative) strengths and difficulties for each child. We aim to accomplish that by developing touch-screen games that measure cognitive, emotional and social processes in children and young people of all ages and abilities. We are currently working on three applications, each intended for a different age group.

Pip’s World is intended for pre-schoolers who are between the ages of 3 and 6. It follows the life of Pip and his friends and includes 10 fun games for the youngest children.

Time Trekkers is an application for school-age children who are between the ages of 6 and 11. Theo, the main character in the game, has built a special time machine that can transport your child to different worlds.

What can I do to help?

Click on the tabs corresponding to different age ranges to find out more about studies that we are currently conducting. Your help will be very beneficial to develop our tasks further.
